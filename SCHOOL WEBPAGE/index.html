Science Fair

This event has a objective of exploring the world of science and give a experience to those who chose to join in this kind of events.
You should go to the science fair because it’s a fun and exciting way to learn about science through hands-on experiments and creative projects.
It gives you a chance to explore topics you’re curious about and share your ideas with others.
You can also develop important skills like problem-solving, communication, and critical thinking.
Plus, it’s a great opportunity to be inspired by your classmates’ work and even win awards!

Aerobatics

This Event has objective of reforming the student to become confident to there skill. and Improve there Physical Fitness to become more flexible, balance, coordination, and cardiovascular.
You should do aerobatic dance because it helps improve your strength, flexibility, and coordination in a fun and energetic way. It’s a great way to stay active while also expressing yourself through creative movements. Performing aerobatic dance can boost your confidence and teach you discipline as you practice and perfect your routine. Plus, it’s an exciting way to work as a team and perform in front of others!
